Exchanging keys

$ gpg --list-keys

to see your public keyring

$ gpg --output alice.gpg --export alice@email.com

to export your a public key

$ gpg --import alice.gpg

to add a public key to your keyring

Encrypting and decrypting documents

Encrypting a document: 

$ gpg --output doc.gpg --encrypt --recipient
blake@cyb.org doc

Decrypting a document: 

$ gpg --output doc --decrypt doc.gpg
